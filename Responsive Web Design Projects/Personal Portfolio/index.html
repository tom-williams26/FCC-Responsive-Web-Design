About me

In 2015, I decided to further my education and take a course in Information Technology. My passion for technology was rapidly growing and was eager to learn more about the subject. With the support from my lecturers, I was able to achieve exceptionally well in my grades which set me up nicely when applying to universities.

Shortly after starting university, I quickly discovered what was expected from me. There was multiple occasions where I found myself overwhelmed from the course content and there was several times I wanted to drop out of university, but I persevered and unofficially graduated (COVID-19) with a Upper Second-Class Honours.

Towards the end of my Intergrated Masters at univeristy, I have continously been doing research and read how others have made their way into the tech industry, This involved attending local events and engaging with the community organised through the "Meetup" app. Other means of research was through the use of browsing the web and being inspired by the journeys other people have undertaken through the use of Medium, Reddit & youtube.

I am a strong believer in reinforced learning, throughout my univeristy experience I had very little exposure to web development and decided to take an invaluable online course with FreeCodeCamp, Upon completion of this course I believe I will hold the skills and experience to get hired and apply them to real-world projects.
